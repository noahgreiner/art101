Lab 4 - Pseudocoding & Problem Solving

Challenge

The challenge of the lab was to create psuedocode for an everyday task and for a simple computer game. The pseudocode was meant to get us in the habit of thinking in pseudocode terms before jumping into code.

Problems

What problems arose? How did you solve them? No particular problems arose for this lab. It was really helpful to work with a lab partner with different experience.
